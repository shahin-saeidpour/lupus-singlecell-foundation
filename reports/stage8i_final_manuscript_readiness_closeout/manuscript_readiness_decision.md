# Stage 8I manuscript-readiness decision

## Decision

`not_ready_for_strong_final_claims`

## Reason

Stage 8H was blocked because Stage 8G did not provide the required direct baseline input package.

## Q2 readiness

`conditional_limited_readiness`

A cautious internal-methods manuscript may be possible only if it clearly states that the current evidence is internal, baseline-incomplete, and not externally scored.

## Q1 readiness

`not_ready`

A strong Q1 submission claim remains blocked until the project has:

- a real direct donor-level pseudobulk baseline;
- a single canonical matched rerun;
- row-level predictions for both primary and baseline methods;
- matched metrics and intervals;
- artifact hashes;
- external or frozen-artifact scoring.

## Allowed current framing

The current work may be framed as an internal validation hardening package that identified and formalized the remaining evidence gaps for manuscript readiness.

## Prohibited current framing

The current work must not be framed as a final validated clinical predictor, a completed baseline comparison, or an externally validated result.
