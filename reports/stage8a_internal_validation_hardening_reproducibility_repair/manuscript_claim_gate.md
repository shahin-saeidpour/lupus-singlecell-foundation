# Manuscript claim gate

## Current claim status

The current internal result is promising but not yet hardened enough for strong Q1-style claims.

## Q2-ready conditions

A cautious Q2-style manuscript claim becomes more defensible when:

- canonical CV metrics are regenerated under one protocol;
- row-level prediction table is available;
- fold stability is reported;
- artifact hashes are recorded;
- at least one direct pseudobulk or raw-expression baseline is included.

## Q1-ready conditions

A Q1-style claim requires all Q2 conditions plus stronger evidence, such as:

- robust confidence intervals under repeated donor-level CV;
- strong comparison against conventional baselines;
- external proxy transfer execution or another independent validation strategy;
- clear biological interpretation or cell-type contribution analysis;
- public reproducibility package.

## Current blocking decision

Do not write a strong headline claim until Stage 8A repair outputs are executed and verified.

## Safe current framing

Frozen foundation-model embeddings show promising donor-level separation of active SLE flare from managed SLE in the internal cohort, but further reproducibility hardening and baseline comparison are required before strong generalization claims.
