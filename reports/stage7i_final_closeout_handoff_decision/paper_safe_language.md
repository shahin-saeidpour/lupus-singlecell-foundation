# Paper-safe language for Stage 7

## Safe wording

GSE135779 was identified as a technically feasible public lupus single-cell cohort for external feasibility assessment.

Supplementary Table 1b provided sample-level SLEDAI metadata, enabling design of a restricted proxy transfer endpoint comparing high-activity SLE with low-activity SLE.

The approved proxy endpoint is SLEDAI > 4 versus SLEDAI <= 4.

External scoring was not performed because frozen Stage 6 model artifacts and external sample embeddings were not yet available in a reproducible form.

## Unsafe wording

Do not write that the Stage 6 model was externally validated on GSE135779.

Do not write that GSE135779 validates FLARE versus managed SLE.

Do not write that SLEDAI <= 4 is the same as managed SLE.

Do not report external performance metrics until real scoring has been performed with frozen artifacts and no external-label tuning.

## Final manuscript position

Stage 7 supports an honest feasibility and proxy-transfer design statement, not a headline external-validation claim.
